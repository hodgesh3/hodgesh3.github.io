Patient Centered Medical Home

Patient Centered Medical Home In 2012, Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan designated Third Coast Family Practice as a Patient Centered Medical Home. They have re-affirmed this designation annually for the past 4 years. A Patient Centered Medical Home (PCMH) is a national model of care that commits to tenants of high quality such as:

  • being accessible when patients need you
  • monitoring and improving the quality of care delivered for individual patients and for groups of patients with serious conditions such as diabetes
  • proactively tracking and communicating results of tests or consultations
  • serving as a resource for patients with education and links to community resources
